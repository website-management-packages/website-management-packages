SSL certificates are one of the most basic yet crucial aspects of site security services within a website management package. An SSL certificate encrypts data transmitted between the user's browser and the website, ensuring that sensitive information such as payment details, login credentials, and personal information is securely transmitted. With an SSL certificate, your website’s URL begins with "https://", which signals to visitors that the site is secure and trustworthy.
